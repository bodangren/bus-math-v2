# Project Sprint: Finalize & Rehearse

## Phase 1: Goal Setting
- **Goal:** Define daily milestones and plan work.
- **Activity:** Review the Submission Checklist. Each team lists their final three tasks to complete (e.g., 'Check spelling', 'Export as PDF', 'Final pitch rehearsal').

## Phase 2: Workshop / Studio Time
- **Goal:** Dedicated time for students to build their projects.
- **Activity:** Group Work Day. Finalize the Balance Sheet, ledger package, and pitch. Ensure all naming conventions (U01L10_TeamName_FinalBS.xlsx) are followed.

## Phase 3: Milestone Checkpoint
- **Goal:** Verify progress against the day's goals.
- **Activity:** Milestone Checkpoint. The teacher performs a final spot-check on the submission package. Every team must show their exported PDF version of the Balance Snapshot.

## Phase 4: Peer Feedback & Reflection
- **Goal:** Get input from peers and reflect on the day's work.
- **Activity:** Reflection. Each student completes a personal reflection on their project contribution and what they've learned about the importance of 'Balance by Design'.

---

## Teacher Notes
- **Pedagogy:** Maintain high standards for the final product. Professionalism and clarity are key today.
- **Setup:** Ensure the submission link (e.g., Google Classroom, Canvas) is active and instructions are clear.
