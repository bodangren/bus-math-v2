# Project Sprint: Refine & Document

## Phase 1: Goal Setting
- **Goal:** Define daily milestones and plan work.
- **Activity:** Review the Project Brief. Each team must list three specific goals for today (e.g., 'Categorize all 20 new rows', 'Fix any SUMIF errors', 'Complete the peer-review checklist').

## Phase 2: Workshop / Studio Time
- **Goal:** Dedicated time for students to build their projects.
- **Activity:** Group Work Day. Teams work on their master ledger. Use Excel comments to explain tricky categorizations. Maintain version history by saving incremental drafts (v1.1, v1.2).

## Phase 3: Milestone Checkpoint
- **Goal:** Verify progress against the day's goals.
- **Activity:** Equation Check. Does your master team ledger balance? Every team must show a 'TRUE' equation check cell before moving to feedback.

## Phase 4: Peer Feedback & Reflection
- **Goal:** Get input from peers and reflect on the day's work.
- **Activity:** Peer Review. Swap laptops with another team. Use the Peer-Review Checklist to flag any miscategorized accounts or formatting issues.

---

## Teacher Notes
- **Pedagogy:** Act as a consultant. Walk around and listen to the 'why' behind student categorizations.
- **Setup:** Ensure the Project Brief and Peer-Review Checklist are distributed.
